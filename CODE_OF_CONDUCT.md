# Contributor Code of Conduct

## Diversity and Inclusivity

In order to foster and maintain an open, friendly and fruitful environment, we as contributors and maintainers commit to participate in our project and our community in accordance with principles of diversity and inclusivity. We believe that working together with respect and appreciation of our differences is not only crucial for personal well being and safety, but also better for ideas and innovation. All contributors pledge to make participating in this project a harrasment-free experience for everyone regardless of their gender identity and expression, age, body size, disability, sexual identity and orientation, race, ethnicity, religious beliefs or level of experience.

## Standards and Expected Behaviour

Examples of behaviour that contributes to creating a respectful and enjoyable environment include:

- Using inclusive, respectful, and kind language
- Treating everyone with the upmost respect
- Embracing diversity and inclusion by welcoming different viewpoints and ways of thinking.
- Showing empathy to community members, and being a generous reader (giving persons the benefit of the doubt with their intentions).
- Openness to feedback and constructive criticism.

## Unacceptable Behaviour

Examples of unacceptable behaviour:

- Inappropriate language and actions.
- Any form of discrimination and disrespect.
- Harrassment or bullying towards anyone, publicly or privately
- Sharing private information of others without consent.
- Insulting, demeaning, or derogatory comments.
- Any other conduct that is reasonably considered inappropriate in a professional environment.

## Consequences of Unacceptable Behaviour

Instances of unacceptable behaviour may be reported by contacting the project team. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated in a timely matter. Confidentiality for the reporter of any incident will be carefully and dutifully protected, and enforement will be carefully considered. Depending upon the context of the issue, verbal and written warnings may be issued along with internat investigations into all involved parties. Project contributors and maintainers who are found to be in violation of the code of conduct, may be temporarility or permanently removed by project leadership, especially for severe or repeated offences.

## Procedure for Reporting and Dealing with Unacceptable Behaviour

- Report any issue to one or more of the core group members: Amar Gill, Claire Saunders, Ruth Yankson, Limor Winter.
- A confidential and thorough investigation will be conducted.
- All affected parties will be able to argue for and defend themselves.
- A final decision will be made based on a majority team vote.
